Research

As an aspiring researcher, my goal is to develop data-driven solutions that support sustainable development and make machine learning models more understandable, reliable, and trustworthy.

My contributions include building a method to detect adulterated oil using machine learning, predicting student GPA with interpretable models, and developing a real-time road fault detection system using deep learning technologies.
